About Us

Miracle Caring Aides 2 was founded from the heart and soul of Mrs. Toi Dixson who was born with a caretaker’s spirit. From caring for her own father and then grandmother, Toi has answered the call to help so many more elderly clients remain in their homes during their aging years and enjoy the quality of life everyone wants to enjoy. With 15+ years of experience providing care, Toi has built a company staffed with additional licensed Certified Nursing Assistants (CNA) just like hospitals and nursing facilities. Only in the home environment, your family member receives 100% of their caregiver’s attention and affection.

Our caregivers are carefully screened including not just background checks, but also verification of their training and skills to ensure they are providing a safe and secure experience for the client. We expect our staff to care for our clients the same as they would care for their own family.

As Director of Care, Toi works with families to ensure the needs of the client are fully met, whether by in-home care or by transportation to appointments or even to shop for groceries. The ultimate goal for Miracle Caring Aides 2 is to provide the best care possible to allow clients to remain in their home in the environment where they are comfortable. At Miracle Caring Aides 2, every client becomes family.
